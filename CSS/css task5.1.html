Sri Ramakrishna engineering college

Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College is Reaccredited by NAAC with ‘A+’ grade and Seven of the B.E/ B.Tech programmes have been accredited and re - accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi, since 2003. The institution currently has 4400 plus students passionately mentored by 271 faculty members of whom 104 are holding Ph.D, with 105 pursuing Ph.D A very good research culture exists in the campus with an average annual funding of Rs. 3.4 crore from various funding agencies - AICTE, DST, CSIR, DRDO etc., The institution is also funded through DST- FIST and DST- INSPIRE and DST- NST MIS
