Centre Begins Auction For Telecom Spectrum Worth ₹ 96,238 Crore

The total quantum of spectrum being auctioned is 10,522.35 MHz in various bands, valuing ₹ 96,238.45 crore at reserve prices, said the Ministry of Communications.The following spectrum bands will go up for bidding in the auction -- 800 MHz, 900 MHz, 1800 MHz, 2100 MHz, 2300 MHz, 2500 MHz, 3300 MHz and 26 GHz -- that starts at 10 a.m.
