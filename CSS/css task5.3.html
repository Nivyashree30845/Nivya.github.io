PSG college

PSG College of Technology commonly known as PSGCT was established in 1951 as the first private Engineering Institution in Tamil Nadu by G.R. Damodaran. It is affiliated to Anna University and is located in Coimbatore. PSGCT is ranked 63rd position in the Engineering category & 89th in the Management category by NIRF 2023. Popularly PSGCT offers courses in Science & Technology, Engineering, and Management. B.E. & B.Tech admissions are based on TNEA Counselling. While M.E, M.Tech, MBA, PGDM, and MCA are based on TANCET followed by TANCA Counselling. GATE is also accepted for M.E. and M.Tech. The PhD admissions at the college are based on GATE / NET / JRF / PSGCT Entrance scores.
